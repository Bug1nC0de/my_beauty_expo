South Africa

The B2B event for global cosmetic industry professionals

Why Exhibit?

My Beauty Expo 2024 is not just a major showcase of beauty products, but also the showcase of beauty and wellness trends, beauty services, packaging and labelling solutions.

Therefore, at MBE 2024, you can be assured to:

  • Expand your market share by meeting new buyers and prospects
  • Launch/promote your latest products and services to targeted buyers
  • Gain competitive advantage through peer networking on the latest industry happenings
  • Reinforce business relationships through face to face interactions
  • Boost sales of your existing products and services
  • Showcase yourself on an International Platform
  • Cutting Edge topics covering beauty solutions
  • Latest Research & Technology on Human Longevity
  • International Trends, Products and Technology
  • Application of Advanced Scientific, Medical and holistic Offerings
  • Present a range of Anti-Ageing Solutions – Lifestyle, holistic, natural, non-surgical, traditional & Cosmetic
