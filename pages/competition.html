Expo

My Beauty 2024

Will enable small and medium size businesses to present to the local and international market their products and trends and to achieve even greater visibility with local and international distributors and retailers.

Makeup competition

MBE will launch its first International & Local make up competition, workshops and make-up demos where you can learn, watch, shop and meet your favourite make up artist and beauty influences.

MBE aims to host around 350 African and 250 International exhibitors, MBE 2024 will be held under one roof at the Durban ICC and SCC, Johannesburg. The events will feature a hybrid format, running a parallel digital platform available for all stakeholders unable to travel to South Africa.

Barber competition

MBE aims to bring people across the globe to come experience the first ever MBE 2024 Barber competition. Industry leaders shared their knowledge, while barbers showcased their skills in the battles.
