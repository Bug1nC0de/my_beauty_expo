The Annual My Beauty Expo

Experience the pinnacle of beauty and wellness at the South African International My Beauty Expo (MBE), Kwa-Zulu Natal’s premier marketing event in the industry. Our debut in 2019 was nothing short of spectacular, earning acclaim as a triumph for our sponsors, over 60 exhibitors, and more than 2500+ attendees who flooded the state-of-the-art Sandton Convention Centre. Spanning three exhilarating days, MBE was a showcase of beauty in all its forms.

MBE is not just an event; it's a convergence of the beauty industry's brightest – from seasoned professionals to emerging talents. We gather an eclectic mix of local and international beauty product suppliers, cutting-edge designers, fashion trendsetters, influential educators, and dynamic influencers.

This is your chance to immerse in a world where beauty meets business, to market your brand, and to seize unparalleled opportunities in the booming multi-billion-dollar beauty sector, where South Africa is a key player.

Join us at MBE, where beauty is not just seen but experienced in its most glorious forms.
