My beauty expo 2024

South Africa

Visitors will have a unique opportunity to meet and interact with companies
who represent the products they admire so greatly,

My Beauty Expo provides you with the opportunity for business expansion,
networking and learning in your sector. Through the convenient hybrid platforms,
you will be able to source the latest products and innovations,
find solutions to current challenges and network with key stakeholders both face-to-face
and digitally at our credible events.

The Hybrid event held at SCC and ICC Centre means
in-person buyers will maximize their time by sourcing from various product
sectors all in one venue. Product sectors include finished
products categories of Cosmetics & Toiletries,
Beauty Salon, Nails, Natural & Organic,
Hair and the new areas "Halal & Bumiputera’s” as MBE 2024
will host suppliers from Ingredients & Lab,
Contract Manufacturing, Primary & Secondary Packaging, Prestige Pack & OEM,
Print & Label, Machinery & Equipment.
